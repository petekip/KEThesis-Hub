Thesis Hub is an online thesis management and monitoring system of all research studies of students from BISU Bilar. It stored the title of the study, abstract and the author.

Features

  1. Search with year and category.

  2. Allowed upload of pdf and images taken during the conduct of study with caption per pdf or image.

  3. View or download pdf and image file

  4. List and graphical view of report daily, weekly monthly and yearly visitors.

  5. Mapping with details of places where the study is conducted.

  6. And more graphical (bar chart, pie chart and line graph) view.

  7. Some of the feature that can be useful in accreditation of the University is not be public yet while the sutdy is on going. You may contact me.

User Guide

How to post new thesis?

  1. Login in your account with administration privilege

  2. Click Administration in menu bar.

  3. In administration area click Post in left side menu bar

  4. Find/click the Add new button

  5. In Abstract form Input all required information in the form field then click next.

  6. In Author You may click Skip button to continue to next form or use the header tab (Abstract,Author, Approval , File ,Complete) to go to next form or click to Complete tab to skip all form add new post.

How to edit posted thesis?

  1. Login in your account with administration privilege

  2. Click Administration in menu bar.

  3. In administration area click Post in left side menu bar

  4. Find/click the Edit button at the last column in list of post.

  5. In Abstract form Input all required information in the form field then click next.

  6. In Author You may click Skip button to edit other form or use the header tab (Abstract, Author, Approval , File) to edit other form.
